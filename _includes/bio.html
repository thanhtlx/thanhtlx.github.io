Short Bio

I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Software Engineering at the VNU University of Engineering and Technology (UET), with an expected graduation date of June 2026. I am actively seeking opportunities to continue my academic journey through a PhD program in Computer Science, where I aim to conduct impactful research in AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE).

My primary research focuses on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Software Engineering, with a focus on leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) and machine learning to enhance software reliability, security, and maintainability. Specifically, I develop techniques for vulnerability detection in code changes, automated generation of patch descriptions, empirical evaluation of LLMs’ code semantic understanding, and assessment of LLM-generated code quality. Looking ahead, I aim to advance trustworthy AI-driven software engineering through white-box LLM analysis, machine translation for code, and rigorous frameworks for AI system evaluation.

I have also gained research experience at the iSE Lab at UET, where I strengthened my skills in software analysis, AI model evaluation, and code-centric deep learning.
