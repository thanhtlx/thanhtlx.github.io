Short Bio

I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Software Engineering at the VNU University of Engineering and Technology (UET), with an expected graduation date of June 2026. I am actively seeking opportunities to continue my academic journey through a PhD program in Computer Science, where I aim to conduct impactful research in AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE).

My primary research focuses on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Software Engineering, with a particular emphasis on enhancing the reliability, quality, and interpretability of code generated or processed by Large Language Models (LLMs). Specifically, I am interested in developing novel techniques for automated code quality assessment and correctness evaluation by exploiting internal representations and dynamic attention mechanisms within code LLMs.

I have also gained research experience at the iSE Lab at UET, where I strengthened my skills in software analysis, AI model evaluation, and code-centric deep learning.
